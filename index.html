Biography

I am a Research Scientist at Google Research. My long-term goal is to scale World Models for ASI. I focus on integrating internet-scale video pretraining with physics-based control to create unified humanoid agents capable of mastering both physical and digital environments.

I am currently exploring the intersection of World Models and System 2 Reasoning, specifically:

  • Unifying large-scale video pretraining with high-fidelity, interactive world models.
  • Bridging generative vision with causal physics priors to enable robust generalization and manipulation.
  • Architecting asynchronous and streaming multimodal frameworks that integrate real-time generation and reasoning within world models.

I am open to discussing the future of MLLMs, Multimodal Generative AI, World Models, and Embodied AI. I welcome high-impact collaborations across industry and academia. Authorized to work in the US and Australia.
